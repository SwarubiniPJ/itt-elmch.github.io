Pain Points Solutions
There is no well-established in vivo marker of nigral degeneration in Parkinson's disease Neuron-derived α-synuclein in blood test: Pathologically folded αsynuclein accumulates in the brain of PD patients. A new test detects this protein also in neuronal extracellular vesicles contained in the patient’s blood. In future, this may help to distinguish between PD patients and healthy individuals.

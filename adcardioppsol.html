Pain point & solution analysis

Pain Points Solutions
Patients with sedentary diseases such as type 2 diabetes and cardiovascular disease are at an increased risk for AD. National screening efforts:Screening for sedentary diseases in form of preventive health check-ups of at-risk groups can aid in early detection and timely management of these diseases.
Wearables for disease detection: Already today, wearable sensors e.g., in form of smart watches, measure and analyze various health parameters. This can be employed to detect and monitor diabetes or cardiovascular disease.

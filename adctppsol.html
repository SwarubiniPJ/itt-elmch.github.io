Pain point & solution analysis

Pain Points Solutions
Patients who experience a stroke are at a higher risk of developing AD. This risk seems to increase by severity and frequency of the experienced stroke(s). Improved treatment workflow: Improved stroke care workflows are integrating new ways of data handling, to empower data-driven decision making and to better connect care teams and patients, with powerful and time-saving imaging technology such as the syngo DynaCT. Also enabling angio-only workflows that combine CT-capable angiography-suite with IV lysis and thrombectomy to reduce door-to-reperfusion-time significantly.

Pain Points Solutions
DBS requires anesthesia, surgical incisions, and brain implants. It therefore carries surgical risks such as the risk for infections. Exablate neuro: This technology that can be used as an alternative to DBS, uses non-invasive MR-guided ultrasound to precisely target, heat and ablate parts of the globus pallidus which is overactive in PD patients. Thereby, motor symptoms such as rigor and tremor are reduced. Infection risk is also lower with this method

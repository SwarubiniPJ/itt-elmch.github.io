Pain point & solution analysis

Pain Points Solutions
The complexity of MS requires a huge amount of MRI data. However, conventional mapping methods are relatively time – consuming and typically measure only one tissue property at a time. MR Fingerprinting (MRF) : MRF is a new approach to MRI scanning measuring multiple tissue properties in a single, time-efficient acquisition – enabling clinicians to make more reliant assessment of tissue characteristics and lesions
Conventionally, physicians and radiologists rely on experience and manual pattern recognition when evaluating MRI images, which could be time-consuming and prone to error. AI supported imaging: AI algorithms can help significantly to evaluate MRI scans with improvements in the detection and segmentation of lesions, early detection of disease and prognosis for MS patients, thereby reducing the time taken for image analysis by radiologists
The course of the disease in a single person can vary significantly over time also considering the number and size of inflammatory lesions. This makes judgements about the progression of MS difficult AI based progress tracking tool in MRI: Progress tracking plug-in as an additional feature within MRI system that automatically tracks the progression and compares the patient’s MRI scan images over time. This helps clinicians assess the treatment response.
FLAIR imaging:FLAIR image fusion for follow-up in MS patients allows for faster and more precise patient care and has better specificity for detecting changes in lesion load and volume

Telemedicine Department

Pain point & solution analysis

To treat PD sufficiently, frequent doctor and therapist visits are necessary. However, on one hand low income countries lack resources and access to qualified PD specialists; on the other hand, patients might have physical difficulties to leave the house
Pain Points Solutions
Telemedicine: Telemedicine such as real-time, synchronous video-conferencing combined with wearables enables geographically isolated or homebound patients to receive improved and restructured PD care.
Smartphone apps: PD patients need specific training to combat their motor and non-motor symptoms. There are several apps supporting this effort providing speech training or physical activity plans and instructions tailored to PD patients needs
VR for Parkinson's patients: VR is a powerful tool to help PD patients enhance motivation and repetition of exercises and provide precise feedback that has an additional positive effect on the motor abilities of patients.
